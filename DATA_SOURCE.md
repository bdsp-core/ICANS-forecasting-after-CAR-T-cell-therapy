# Data source & provenance — Forecasting ICANS after CAR-T

## Paper
Amidi Y, Eckhardt CA, Quadri SA, Malik P, Firme MS, Jones DK, Jain A, Danish HH,
Rubin DB, Jacobson CA, Cash SS, Lee JW, Dietrich J, **Westover MB**.
*Forecasting immune effector cell-associated neurotoxicity syndrome after chimeric
antigen receptor t-cell therapy.* **J Immunother Cancer 2022 Nov;10(11):e005459.**
doi:10.1136/jitc-2022-005459 · PMID 36450377 · PMCID PMC9716920.

Cohort: 199 patients treated with CAR-T at Brigham and Women's Hospital
(Apr 2015 – Feb 2020). Combined hidden Markov model + lasso-penalized logistic
regression, leave-one-patient-out cross-validation.

## Committed data (de-identified — the analysis runs entirely from this)
- **`AllBWH_5days_deID.xlsx`** — one row per patient-hospital-day. Columns:
  `SID` (surrogate patient id), `Age`, `Sex`, `Diagnosis`, `Subtype`, `HospitalDay`,
  daily `Temp`, `CRP`, `Ferritin`, `Procalcitonin`, `IL_6`, `WBC`, the ICANS
  `meanscore` (0–4), and CRS summary fields. **No MRN, no names, no dates.**
- **`data_new.mat`** — `Y` (199×100 ICANS-grade trajectories), `Ya` (augmented
  states for smoother transition matrices, `rng(10)`), `Z` (emission states).
  Regenerated from `AllBWH_5days_deID.xlsx` by `build_data_new.m`.
- **`LR_prob_allp_Lasso_LOO.mat`** — `yfit`, the per-patient leave-one-out
  lasso-LR onset probabilities. Regenerated by `NTonset_PLR.m`.
- **`time_LR.mat`** — `s`, the fixed time-of-onset risk curve (peaks ~day 9);
  a deterministic function of day, not patient data.

## PHI — NOT in this repo
The raw export **`AllBWH_5days_updated01172022.xlsx`** contains `MRN`, `zID`
(Z-numbers), `LastName`, and `FirstName` — direct identifiers. It is **git-ignored
and never published.** De-identification replaced `MRN` with a rank-preserving
surrogate `SID` (identical `unique()` grouping/order ⇒ identical results — verified
in MATLAB: the de-identified file reproduces the raw `Y` grade matrix **exactly**,
max abs diff = 0) and dropped `zID`/`LastName`/`FirstName`. The `SID↔MRN` crosswalk
lives only in Box `Brandon - PHI/crosswalks/CROSSWALK_forecast_SID_to_MRN.csv`.

**PHI-scanned 2026-07-07:** raw = 205 patient identities (MRN/zID/names) → de-id = 0.

## Lineage
raw BWH EDW export (`AllBWH_5days_updated01172022.xlsx`, MRN-keyed)
→ de-identify (MRN→`SID`, drop names/zID) → `AllBWH_5days_deID.xlsx`
→ `build_data_new.m` → `data_new.mat` (Y/Ya/Z)
→ `NTonset_PLR.m` → `LR_prob_allp_Lasso_LOO.mat` (yfit)
→ `Forecast_Matricies.m` / `time_to_end.m` → forecast metrics & figures.
